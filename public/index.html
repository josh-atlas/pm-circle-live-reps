How It Works
01The Hot Seat

Two participants answer a live prompt. Everyone else scores on structure, insight, and delivery — then compares notes with coach feedback.

02Practice Reps

Between sessions: write out your answer, then record a 10-minute video response. Optional sharing in the group WhatsApp from session 3 onward.

03Direct Coaching

Feedback is specific, not generic. You'll know exactly where you lost the interviewer and exactly what to change next time.
